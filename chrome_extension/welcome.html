Getting Started

Catalyst Whisper Coach analyzes your conversations across multiple platforms and provides real-time coaching to improve your communication. Here's how to get started:

1

Sign In to Your Account

Click the Catalyst icon in your browser toolbar and sign in with the same credentials you use on the Catalyst web platform.

Don't have an account yet? Visit our website to create one.
2

Enable for Your Messaging Platforms

The extension works with these platforms:

WhatsApp
Messenger
Instagram
Discord
Slack
Teams
Telegram

By default, all platforms are enabled. You can customize this in the extension settings.

3

Start Chatting

Open any supported messaging platform and start a conversation. The extension will automatically analyze your messages and provide coaching suggestions in real-time.

For privacy reasons, the extension only analyzes messages when you're actively chatting and doesn't store the full content of your conversations.

How It Works

Real-time Analysis

The extension monitors your conversation and analyzes:

  • Sentiment and emotional tone
  • Communication patterns
  • Potential misunderstandings
  • Opportunities for deeper connection

Whisper Suggestions

Based on the analysis, you'll receive "whispers" - coaching suggestions that appear in a small widget. These whispers help you:

  • Respond more effectively
  • Navigate difficult conversations
  • Express yourself more clearly
  • Build stronger connections

Privacy-Focused

Your privacy is important to us:

  • Message content is processed securely
  • You can enable "Privacy Mode" to limit data sent to servers
  • No full conversation logs are stored
  • You control which platforms are monitored

Customizing Your Experience

Click the Catalyst icon and go to Settings to customize:

  • Platforms: Enable or disable specific messaging platforms
  • Analysis Frequency: How often the extension analyzes messages
  • Whisper Settings: How suggestions appear and how long they stay visible
  • Privacy Mode: Limit data sent to servers for more privacy

Ready to Get Started?

Click the Catalyst icon in your browser toolbar to open the extension and sign in.
